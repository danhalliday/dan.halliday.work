Graham has been operating his school of motoring in and around the Midlands area for years, but without any contact information available on the internet. The simple site now enables Graham's future driving pupils to find his phone number and hourly rate – just by glancing at the sign on his car! Visit Graham's Website.

Top Hat Property Services is a painting and decorating company operating in central London. They had listings all over the internet but needed a website and logo to smarten up their image and provide one place for information and online quotes. The newest design shows the staff's Twitter posts and fully incorporates their Tumblr blog. View Top Hat Property Services.

Having just mixed the jazz orchestra's album The Horn Ultimatum and spent time with the band, I was in a great position to assess what they needed from a website. I added a lick of paint to the band's neon sign logo, and coded an easy-to-use system for the band leader to log in and update the next rehearsal and other information. The site also features a music player, and integration with Flickr for photos and Vimeo for videos. Visit wyjo.co.uk
